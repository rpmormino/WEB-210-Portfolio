Projects

COOKBOOK

For DME 115, a graphic design tools intro class, I created an 8 page cookbook with three two-page recipe spreads.

END-TIMES MURAL

I designed this mural to use leftover paint from a previous mural. Because of the limited amount of paint, I stuck to primary colors to prevent waste from mixing.

The folkart design was inspired by the limited color palette.

PLEASURE ISLAND REDESIGN

For WEB 125, a responsive web design class, we were tasked with re-designing the site for Pleasure Island.

Taking inspiration from the 70's, when Pleasure Island was named, I created a mobile-first design that is fun and friendly.
